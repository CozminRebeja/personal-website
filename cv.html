About Me

My design career started when I was 15 years old. My cousin showed me a mind-blowing program at the time called Photoshop. It hooked me instantly, so I decided to try it out myself. Through online platforms like YouTube, I studied the ins and outs of graphic design. And by the time I was 16, I got a job at a local firm in Chisinau. Five years later, I am working as a freelance UI/UX Designer, collaborating with other talented people to create stunning visuals and solve real-life problems.
